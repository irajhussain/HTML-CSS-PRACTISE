    General Information

    The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago`s northside neighbourhoods an environment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighbourhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and reliese on the generous Volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
